Rucker's Wine & Spirits

  • Red Wine

    Merlot: A smooth and fruity wine with flavors of black cherry and plum. Perfectly pairs with red meat and pasta dishes.

  • White Wine

    Sauvignon Blanc: A crisp and refreshing wine with citrus and tropical fruit notes. Goes well with seafood and salads.

  • Rosé Wine

    Provence Rosé: A light and dry wine with flavors of strawberry and melon. Ideal for sipping on a sunny day or pairing with light appetizers.

  • Sparkling Wine

    Champagne: An elegant and effervescent wine with notes of apple and toast. Perfect for celebrations and toasts.

  • Whiskey

    Scotch Whiskey: A rich and smoky whiskey with hints of peat and oak. Best enjoyed neat or on the rocks

  • Draft Beers

    Draft Beer: Draft beer typically has a frothy, foamy head on top, which is a result of the carbonation and pouring process.
